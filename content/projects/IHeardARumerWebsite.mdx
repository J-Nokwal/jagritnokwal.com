**I Heard A Rumor (IHAR)** is a social media platform where users can share **rumors, thoughts, or information** in the form of **text or image posts**. Designed as a personal project, the platform allows users to either stay **anonymous** or log in via **Google**.

Built with **React** and powered by **Firebase**, IHAR supports rich features such as:

- Posting text and image-based rumors
- Deep linking to specific posts
- Google login & anonymous access
- Upvoting and commenting on posts
- Fully responsive UI and optimized UX

This project emphasizes anonymous expression and community-driven engagement, encouraging open-ended discussion in a safe space.
